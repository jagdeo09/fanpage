Brady Tutton

Home About Gallery

From Shorewood, Wisconsin, Brady Patrick Tutton was born on December 7, 2018. Before coming on the ABC show, BoyBand, he is a trained opera singer, as well as an actor. He played "Brock" on the ABC sitcom Fresh Off the Boat in 2015. Tutton is also the lead actor in the 2018 movie, The Rocket. For his BoyBand audition, he sang, "On Bended Knee" by Boyz II Men. He went through the ten-week competition without being placed in the bottom two and/or three.
